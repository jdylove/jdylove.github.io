文摘2-规矩的力量

天空中，阴云层层，春雨缠绵地下着。

细如牛毛的细雨，洒落下来，将青茅山都笼罩上一层薄烟。

客栈中的第一层饭堂，仍旧是冷冷清清，只有四桌客人。

方源坐的位置正靠着窗户边，一阵风吹来，夹杂着诗意和花香。

“天街小雨润如酥，草色遥看近却无。”方源遥望窗口，轻轻低吟了一句，这才收回视线。

他面前的桌上摆着好酒好菜，色香味俱全。尤其是青竹酒，酒香香醇又透着一股清新。碧绿色的酒液静静地盛在竹杯中，从这个角度看去，散着琥珀般的温润光泽。

最靠近他的一桌上，是祖孙二人。穿着朴素的衣服，都是凡人。

老爷子一边嘬着米酒，一边羡慕看着方源这边。显然是被青竹酒香勾动，却没有钱财来买。

孙子则吃着卤汁香豆，咬在嘴里咔蹦卡蹦的作响。同时又纠缠着他的爷爷，摇晃着老人的膀子：“爷爷，爷爷，给我讲讲人祖的故事吧。你要是不讲，我就回去告诉奶奶，说你出来偷酒喝！”

“唉，吃个酒都不安稳。”老爷子叹了一口气，脸上却浮现出慈爱之色。他用枯瘦如柴的手摸摸孙子的脑袋，“那我就讲讲吧。话说那人祖把心交给了希望蛊，脱离了困境的围捕……”

人祖的故事，是这个世界流传最广，也是最古老的传说。

老人说的故事，大概是这样子的。

the struture is the most basic, the integtor block is ideal:

and the performance is 64.8dB

Type-I block digital filtering

The block digital filtering has facilitated the design and construction of a true TI ∆Σ modulator, traditional SDM is single input and single output, the block digital filtering helps it to become a MIMO system:


some paper inculdes the k-factor,The k-factor is used to deal with the issue of opamp DC offsets. DC offsets are problematic in TISDM because the difference in offset between the two branches drives the modulator to instability. Reducing the cross-coupling coefficients gives more control to each parallel ∆Σ modulator, thus enabling the negative feedback loop to adjust, which maintains DC stability.

However, reducing k from unity modifies the STF and results in an increase of the quantization noise in the signal band, thereby reducing the SNDR. The choice of k is a tradeoff between the offset value that the modulator can tolerate and the achievable SNR.

we can see 2-path means we double the sampling frequency, and the SNR performance increases 15dB as we expected, because here we just use ideal components, so we didn’t put the k-factor into count.

the directly 2-path

the newbie may think repeating single path is call TI-SDM, yes, it is, but it’s not just easy. for a reminder, the 2-path wil increase $$$z$$$ to $$$z^{2}$$$, so as usually peopele use such kind SDM as a bandpass one. for example, you can easily find 4-path TI-SDM locating the center frequency at fs/2. what’s more, if you look at the NTF:
$$NTF=(1-z^{-2})^{2}=(1-z^{-1})^{2}(1+z^{-1})^{2}$$
therefore, a plain two-path second-oder scheme experiences a loss in SNR of 12dB. and the simuation result poroves it.

and we can notice the pwd has a drop at fs/2, which also proves the bandpass feature of this kind TI-SDM.

Type-II output prediction scheme

one problem of TI-SDM is the hardware doubles as well as the mismatch problem occurs. and one special problem for TI-SDM is the quantitizer dumino, which means the output of one quantitizer can not be put into input of another quantitizer directly. this is due to the recursive operation of SDM.

however, we can use an incomplete integrator output for the input of the second quantizer in order to eliminate the quantizer domino. Incomplete integrator output is the complete integrator output without the modulator output terms.

the drawback is the large number of levels for the second quantizer(the performance usually drops) and the global feedback DAC(not undertand)



the SNR=77.9dB,and we have 3dB loss, not has good as we expected, and I am looking for the reason, I think it may due to the stability.

supplement

1. ZI-TI

A high-speed demux is needed at the input of the modulator to sample the input-signal and distribute it to the individual internal modulators. The demux operates at the full speed of the overall TI structure. For example, for a time-interleaving factor of 4, the demux operates at four times the speed of the individual ∆Σ modulators.

A solution to the demux problem for J=2 is to sample each branch at a different phase of the two non-overlapping clocks. Therefore, the demux is inherent in the operation of the modulator. Another more general solution that can be used for any J is called the zero-insertion interpolation technique, it keeps one input J times the original imput power, and leaves other inputs as zero. for example, we use this tech on the block digital filtering TI-SDM:


and by arranging the quantitizer outputs, the perfomance losses 2dB, becoming 79.1dB, which is still acceptable. so it has two problems:

  1. The input signal amplification causes the ZITI modulator to become unstable at smaller input levels than the traditional TI modulator, because we will increase power at one input!
  2. The ZITI modulator still suffers from DC offsets, so I think it’s not so suitable for low-pass case unless the DC offset tone is out of band of your interests.

梦里，我常回到离高考还有半年或者一年那么短的时间，然后忧虑自己能不能考上一所好大学。有时又好像觉得自己似乎读过大学，梦里明明身在高中却担忧自己能不能本科毕业，没有丝毫逻辑可言。碰到考试，对着空白的试卷一道题也不会。突然一觉惊醒，躺在床上面对黑暗的房间，我才意识到自己已经毕业好些年了。想到梦里那些担忧多是多余的，不禁狂喜。想想过往，那些年念书也受了不少苦，又感到慰藉。总之，看那个小孩就好像看别人家孩子似的，对他的坚强有一种顾影自怜的爱意。放在今天，打死都不肯早上五点多冒着风雪起床，深一脚浅一脚地踩着积雪走到学校上早读。然后不禁感叹：我小时候真是好学生。
